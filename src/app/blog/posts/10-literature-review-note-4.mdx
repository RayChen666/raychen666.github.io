## Summary

From the [meeting](https://raychen666.vercel.app/blog/9-meeting-note-3#graph-based-data-structure) on 
04/03/26 we identified that for the LLM to better understand the spatial relationships among objects it would be 
better for us to speciﬁcally design the SceneGraph data pipeline for the spatial memory module. However, this is a huge amount of the 
work and not the main research part for this project, so instead, we are seeking for some relevant articles or some 
open-source frameworks that can be directly used for the SceneGraph. Above are the relevant sources that I found might be useful 
to our system design & implementation. 

We are unable to review all of the articles in this blog post, so I'll just focus on the **first article** for this literature review.
